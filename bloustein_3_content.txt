Alumni







Alumni of the Bloustein School are distinguished leaders who skillfully solve real-world issues and produce results that matter. We encourage you to use this section to learn more about the work and achievements of your fellow alumni; and keep in touch with them, the Edward J. Bloustein School, and the greater Rutgers community.









Stay Connected

Update your contact information, and stay connected with the Bloustein School community and other alumni! You can also tell us what you’re up to by contacting your class correspondent, joining our Slack instance, a regional alumni group, or our LinkedIn group.






Alumni Groups
Joining an Alumni Group is one of the best ways to create and maintain connections with fellow alumni. Are you moving to a new city? Do you want to share best practices with alumni in your field? Are you just looking to network during a happy hour? We have a place for you!





LinkedIn Group
This group is dedicated to providing alumni of the Bloustein School a way to re-connect with former classmates, colleagues and establish new networking relationships.







Alumni Career Support
Our dedicated career counselor is committed to helping you through every stage of your career, as you evaluate professional goals, develop a plan and pursue opportunities.





Distinguished Alumni Awards
The Bloustein School Alumni Achievement Awards showcase and honor those alumni whose personal and professional accomplishments have been of the highest caliber and brought credit to the school.





Give Back
Whether you want to eliminate taxes or benefit from an increased income stream, there is a gift to fit every objective. And no matter how or what you give, rest assured that you will be helping a cause close to your heart.
