Honors Programs
      


Rutgers honors students benefit from an inclusive integrated honors community, offering richer course options, more extracurricular opportunities, personalized advising, professional and peer mentoring, and access to honors housing in their first year and beyond.

          The Support You Need to Succeed
      


University life can take a little getting used to. We’re always here to help, so just ask. Support is provided even before you arrive on campus, and continues long after graduation.













Learning Centers



Tough class? Need to sharpen skills or deepen understanding? Rutgers Learning Centers can help you develop the capabilities and fundamental approaches to succeed in your coursework.












Counseling Services



The expert staff at Counseling, Alcohol and Other Drug Assistance Program & Psychiatric Services (CAPS) develops an individualized action plan based on the unique needs of each student.












Career Services



Rutgers offers a range of assistance to prepare for success in the working world, from choosing the right path to developing your skills to pursuing your goals wisely and confidently.












School Advising Offices



Take advantage of general academic advising and specialized academic support available through each school's advising office. Rutgers offers guidance in coursework and careers as you move toward your graduation goals.

          Get Your Feet Wet
      


Beyond the books, hands-on learning opportunities can help to better prepare you for the career of your dreams. Made possible by its proximity to the Jersey Shore, Rutgers’ Scientific Diving Program offers undergraduates a certification once reserved for graduate- and PhD-level students. The diving program is just one example of how Rutgers–New Brunswick is providing valuable and highly desirable job skills.
