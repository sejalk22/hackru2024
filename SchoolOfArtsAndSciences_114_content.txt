 Discover a World of Possibilities 


The School of Arts and Sciences (SAS) is the liberal arts school of Rutgers University, the largest and comprehensive school on the New Brunswick campus, and a place of boundless possibilities.
 SAS offers more than 100 majors and minors across the life sciences, humanities, social and behavioral sciences, and mathematical and physical sciences.
 We also offer an honors program, opportunities for undergraduate research, a career exploration program serving all majors, and special Signature Courses taught by our top scholars.
