          Sometimes, Bigger Really Is Better
      


A diverse student body of more than 50,000 with hundreds of majors, 800+ organizations, and virtually limitless resources empower new ideas and drive endless opportunities.




  Start Something Big 





















          Get to Know the Campuses
      











Busch






Busch is home to Rutgers’ high-tech and health schools and research centers, as well as SHI Stadium and an Olympic-sized swimming pool at Werblin Recreation Center.




  Explore Busch Campus 













College Avenue






Historical and architectural marvels abound on College Avenue, dating back to the American Revolution. Today, an Honors College houses 500 top students.




  Explore College Avenue Campus 













Cook






The G.H. Cook residential campus offers a timeless tranquility with its wide-open green spaces, pastoral pond, and even a working organic farm!




  Explore Cook Campus 













Douglass






Take a trip to the past via the colonial architecture and sprawling lawns all around, in addition to Douglass Residential College. This campus is also home to the Global Village living-learning communities.




  Explore Douglass Campus 













Livingston






Anchored by the striking Rutgers Business School building, Livingston’s amenities include a first-run movie theater, Jersey Mike's Arena, plus a 32-acre solar array providing 60% of campus power.




  Explore Livingston Campus 





















The Big Ten
      


Membership in the Big Ten Conference unlocks exclusive opportunities for both students and faculty.















    Collaboration
  





Meaningful partnerships with other influential universities provide access to members-only databases, conferences, and libraries.















    Research
  





The Big Ten Academic Alliance grants membership to an elite group of research universities conducting $10.5B in funded research annually.















    Big Ten Athletics
  





Embrace school pride and cheer on the Scarlet Knights, including 10 Men's and 14 Women's nationally acclaimed NCAA Division One sports teams.

    Our Mission
  


Rutgers University–New Brunswick is dedicated to teaching, research, and service, and is responsible for carrying out the chancellor-provost's bold vision for excellence and equity.








  Learn about our leadership and mission 










    Looking Beyond
  


Rutgers–New Brunswick is poised to deliver on its promise to serve the public; to be an inclusive and intellectually stimulating place where all can flourish; and to transform lives in New Jersey and around the world.





    Focus on Excellence
  


In clinics and centers, in labs and libraries, in the field and across the globe, Rutgers faculty and students generate knowledge, build pathways to success, advance economic progress, improve lives, and enrich humanity.
