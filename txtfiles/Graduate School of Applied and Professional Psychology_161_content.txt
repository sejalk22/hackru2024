The 2024 Graduate School of Applied and Professional Psychology (GSAPP)'s Convocation Ceremony will take place at 11:00 am on Thursday, May 9th, at the Nicholas Music Center, Cook/Douglass Campus. We are so proud of our students and their commitment to reaching this pivotal milestone, a representation of imminent success! 
Regalia Orders
All regalia needs to be ordered from Barnes & Noble at Rutgers: 100 Somerset St., New Brunswick, NJ 08901/(732) 246-8448. Please check website for hours of operation. Pre-order online.
NOTE: Grad Fair will be held at Barnes & Noble at Rutgers on April 4th & 5th from 10:00 am - 6:00 pm.
Portraits
Rutgers has partnered with Pro Pics to take professional portraits of all graduates during the ceremony. Photos will be available for purchase after the ceremony. There is no cost to have your photo taken and no obligation to purchase. If you would like to receive your photo proofs, please go to www.RutgersPhotos.com to submit your information. If you have any questions regarding photos, please contact Pro Pics at info@propicsusa.com.
General Student Information
If you anticipate graduating in May 2024, you must submit a diploma application. Please be sure to fill out the online diploma application by April 3, 2024.
For up-to-date information about Rutgers University-New Brunswick Commencement, taking place on Sunday, May 12th, visit: https://commencement.rutgers.edu/.
