Event Advertising Request Form



If you are planning an in-person event at the Bloustein School, make sure to reserve your room first. Once logistics are in place, or if you have a virtual event, fill out the form below with the event specifics so they may be included on the Bloustein School calendar and lobby displays. 
If your event has a wider scope requiring additional attention such as external advertising or social media, or that may attract media attention, or if you have additional questions, please email webmaster@ejb.rutgers.edu.
