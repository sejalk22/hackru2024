Save the Dates!

On Sunday and Monday, May 12-13, 2024, Rutgers, The State University of New Jersey and the Edward J. Bloustein School of Planning and Public Policy will celebrate our graduates. In addition to Rutgers University–New Brunswick and Rutgers Biomedical and Health Sciences Commencement on Sunday, May 12, 2024 at SHI Stadium, the Bloustein School will hold its own Convocation Ceremony on Monday, May 13, 2024 meant to culminate the experience by recognizing each student personally.







Convocation of the Bloustein School for Graduate and Undergraduate Students



This event scheduled for Monday, May 13, 2024, will begin promptly at 10:00am at Jersey Mike’s Arena on the Livingston Campus.  Check-in for students begins at 8:00 am and closes at 9:00 am.
The formal BLOUSTEIN SCHOOL CONVOCATION ceremony will recognize each graduate individually with pomp and circumstance.  Students will cross the stage and have their names read as they are recognized. Seating is general admission with no tickets and no limits on numbers of family, friends or guests. Parking is ample and available in the Yellow and Green Lots adjacent to Jersey Mike’s Arena.
Important note: GRADUATE students with August 2023, October 2023, January 2024, May 2024 and August 2024 degree dates, and UNDERGRADUATE students with January 2024, May 2024, and August 2024 are invited to participate in the school convocation.
POST-GRADUATION SURVEY: As you embark on new life adventures, we ask that you share your future plans with by completing the Official Post-Graduation Survey with the Rutgers Office of Career Exploration and Success.

Program & Invitation

We look forward to seeing all graduates, their families, and friends on Monday, May 13!
Guest Seating: We have seating for as many guests as you want to bring. Tickets are NOT required.
Have guests that are unable to attend the event in person? Don’t worry – we will be live streaming the event via Facebook Live! They can watch the feed on our website OR view the event directly on Facebook Live.
The live stream will begin at about 9:45 a.m., but the Procession of Graduates will not start until 10:00 a.m. We will do everything we can to ensure the live streaming goes smoothly however, we cannot account for connectivity issues.

Photos

The Bloustein School hires an independent photographer for the Convocation event. Graduates and their families are welcome to download all photos FREE OF CHARGE for personal use. The Bloustein School holds the copyright to all photos. Photos may not be used for any marketing or promotional purposes or without the permission of the subject of the photo and the Bloustein School.
Photos will be posted a few days after the event. All current and past graduations and convocation of the Bloustein School can be found on our website.
For ease of finding the website, we include a QR code on the program available at the in-person event.
