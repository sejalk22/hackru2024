About GSAPP














          Overview
      


Our core values of achieving academic excellence are based on a deep commitment to diversity and inclusion, generating and disseminating knowledge, and promoting social justice in service to underserved populations, all aspects that are foundational to the mission of our world-renowned land grant institution, Rutgers University.
Our faculty are nationally and internationally recognized, committed to publicly engaged scholarship and translating evidence into direct action. Our clinic and center staff are dedicated to providing high quality, high impact, practical experiences to our students and clients. Our students are committed to creating access and addressing barriers to achieve equity. Our alumni are mentors and leaders in local, state, national, and international psychological associations.
We are, “one GSAPP.”
