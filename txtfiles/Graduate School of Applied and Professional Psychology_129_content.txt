          GSAPP’s Grants Office
      


Functions as a central hub to oversee, coordinate, and support researchers on a variety of key tasks.  They provide essential guidance on policies, procedures, procurement/funding, and reporting. Staff work closely with researchers by helping identify funding opportunities, at onset/application, during, and throughout the process.
