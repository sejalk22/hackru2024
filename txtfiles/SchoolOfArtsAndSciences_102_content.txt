          Scarlet Forever
      


The moment you graduate, you belong to the Rutgers University Alumni Association (RUAA), a global network 500,000+ strong. Membership is free for life and includes amazing national, local, and university benefits. Stay connected in whichever ways are meaningful to you.




  Rutgers University Alumni Association 











Rutgers University Alumni Association 






Attend Events
The RUAA hosts a wide variety of special events exclusively for Rutgers alumni. Network with the best and brightest, honor alumni leaders, volunteer to make a difference, and much more.











          Rutgers Day is Back
      


Rutgers Day 2022 is set for Saturday, April 30, 10 a.m. to 4 p.m. on the Busch Campus in Piscataway and the College Avenue and Cook/Douglass campuses in New Brunswick. Reconnect with friends and former classmates and rediscover all things Rutgers.







  Plan Your Day 












Stay Connected
The RUAA website and official social media channels make it easy to update your contact information, reconnect with classmates, and stay in touch with Rutgers. #Rfamily
Access Discounts
Rutgers graduates receive a Rutgers Alumni Card, representing your membership in the RUAA and giving you access to thousands of discounts and services.
Get Involved
There are more than 140 alumni groups across the United States and around the world. Connect with alumni who have similar interests and experiences by exploring RUAA special interest groups, regional clubs, and volunteer activities.
Rutgers Magazine
The universitywide alumni magazine keeps readers connected to their alma mater. Showcasing alumni achievements and university innovations and accomplishments, the magazine is published twice a year in June and November.
Yesterday Meets Today, and Tomorrow
The university is experiencing one of its greatest eras for expansion and enrichment, becoming a Rutgers of unprecedented capacity, excellence, and reach. Your support is essential to keep the momentum going strong.
Here's to the Die-Hards
Danny Klein and his classmates started tailgating as Rutgers students. Thirty years later, they still gather on Saturdays to throw a party and cheer on the Scarlet Knights through good times and bad.

Giving to Rutgers 






From scholarships to chair endowments, annuities to building funding, there are many ways to support Rutgers. Contact the Rutgers University Foundation, whether you’re looking to give back or just give forward.
The Rutgers University Foundation
A diverse student body, a devoted faculty, and a vibrant campus life make Rutgers University–New Brunswick one of the most stimulating educational environments in the United States.
The Rutgers University Foundation works to make it affordable, and your help is what makes it possible.
Alumni, parents, friends, corporations, foundations, and other supporters can contact the foundation and match their interests to Rutgers’ priorities. Learn about ways to make a gift.
"One Person Can Impact the World"
Your support does count. See how one person can impact the world.
