          The Big Picture
      


Rutgers, The State University of New Jersey, is an academic, health, and research powerhouse—with campuses located in New Brunswick, Newark, and Camden—and a statewide academic health leader.



          Watch Video

          New Brunswick
      


Rutgers University–New Brunswick conducts life-changing research and offers premier education in a diverse community of ideas and change-makers to better the world.

          People Are Asking...
      
















When will my financial aid package be available?



Due to changes with the FAFSA process, financial aid packages will be released by the end of April for incoming students and mid to late-June for continuing students.




  Important FAFSA Information 


















Where can I find academic resources?



Find information about academic calendars, transcripts, course catalogs and other academic resources.




  View Academic Resources 


















What immunizations are required?



Immunizations are mandated by New Jersey state law and university policy. Requirements vary by school and program.




  Learn More 


















Where can I search Rutgers jobs?



Rutgers has more than 34,000 employees. In order to deliver on our mission, we're always looking for the best and brightest. We invite you to learn more about working at Rutgers.




  Learn More about Rutgers Jobs
