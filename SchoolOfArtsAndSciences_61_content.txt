          Office of the Chancellor
      


The Office of the Chancellor is responsible for fulfilling the university’s mission of teaching, research, and service at Rutgers–New Brunswick. Central to that mission, Chancellor Francine Conway and her leadership team strive to foster excellence and equity among students, staff, faculty and the entire Rutgers community.

          Leading with Empathy and Compassion
      






          Leading with Empathy and Compassion
      












          Francine Conway, Ph.D.
      



    
    Chancellor of Rutgers–New Brunswick












    Mission-Focused
  


Chancellor Conway leads Rutgers–New Brunswick’s mission of academic excellence, leveraging the institution’s resources and tapping into its potential to be a national leader in student support, faculty enrichment, and community engagement.
She envisions a university where all members–students and faculty–know they belong, and are better able to reach their personal and professional goals.








  Learn about Chancellor Conway 
















Rutgers–New Brunswick is poised to deliver on its promise to serve the public; to be a welcoming and intellectually stimulating place for individuals from diverse backgrounds; and to transform the lives of individuals, communities and the world.
Chancellor Francine Conway

          Planning for the Future
      


The Rutgers–New Brunswick Academic Master Plan, led by the Office of the Chancellor, will serve as the roadmap for the institution’s future, clarifying strategies to accomplish the university’s academic mission.

          The Rutgers Advantage
      


Rutgers–New Brunswick is holistically reimagining the student experience—from enrollment to retention and ultimately graduation—to ensure students are supported in their well-being and sense of belonging, and prepared to succeed in their academic and co-curricular pursuits as well as in the real world.
